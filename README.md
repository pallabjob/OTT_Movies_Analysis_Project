# 🎬 OTT Movies Analysis Dashboard – Interactive Business Insights

## 📌 Project Overview

The OTT Movies Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of movie trends, audience preferences, ratings behavior, language popularity, genre performance, and yearly movie release patterns across OTT platforms. This dashboard helps stakeholders, streaming platforms, content creators, and business analysts understand viewer engagement and strategic content opportunities.

---

# 🌟 Executive Summary

The dashboard reveals that OTT platforms are strongly dominated by high-engagement genres such as Drama, Sci‑Fi, and Crime. The average movie rating of 7.49 indicates that the majority of content available on OTT platforms is positively received by audiences.

With over 251 Million total votes and 2.5K movies analyzed, the dashboard demonstrates significant audience interaction and platform engagement.

The data also highlights increasing movie releases over time, especially after 2023, showing rapid OTT platform expansion and growing digital entertainment consumption.
